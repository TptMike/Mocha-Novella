# Books, Coffee & Music Theme Guide

## Design Philosophy

Your theme is inspired by the cozy atmosphere of a coffee shop, the warmth of well-loved books, and the rhythm of music. It's designed to create an inviting, comfortable space where readers feel at home.

## Color Palette

### Coffee-Inspired Colors
- **Dark Coffee Bean** (#3e2723) - Rich, deep brown for text and strong accents
- **Rich Coffee** (#5d4037) - Primary dark brown
- **Medium Coffee** (#8d6e63) - Primary brand color
- **Light Coffee** (#a1887f) - Lighter accents
- **Amber/Gold** (#d4a574) - Warm highlights (like coffee with cream)

### Paper & Parchment
- **Cream** (#faf8f5) - Main background (like coffee shop walls)
- **Parchment** (#f5f1e8) - Alt background (like aged book pages)
- **White Paper** (#ffffff) - Card backgrounds (like fresh pages)

### Music Accent
- **Music Brown** (#9d6b53) - Subtle music-inspired accent

## Typography

- **Display Font**: Playfair Display (elegant, literary)
- **Body Font**: Georgia (readable serif, book-like)
- **Monospace**: Courier New (typewriter/coffee shop feel)

## Key Features

### Visual Elements
1. **Warm Background**: Cream with subtle radial gradients (like coffee shop lighting)
2. **Book Pages**: Side borders create a book spine effect
3. **Coffee Shop Cards**: Post cards with warm shadows
4. **Musical Notes**: Decorative elements in headers
5. **Cozy Footer**: Dark coffee background with warm accents

### Interactive Elements
- Smooth hover effects
- Coffee cup animation on links
- Warm color transitions
- Inviting button styles

### Reader-Friendly
- Optimal reading width (700px)
- Comfortable line-height (1.8)
- Justified text (book-like)
- Clear typography hierarchy
